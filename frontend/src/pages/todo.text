# ⭐ 2.1 **AI Features (Must Add for Final Year Project)**

Adding AI will make your project FUTURE-READY.

## **🧠 (A) AI Expense Categorization**

When user adds a new expense:

* AI auto-detects category
* Eg.

  * “McDonald’s burger” → *Food*
  * “UBER Ride” → *Transport*

**Implementation:**
Send the description to OpenAI → return predicted category.

---

## **🧠 (B) AI Budget Prediction**

Based on user spending patterns:

* Predict next month’s expense
* Predict how much a user will overspend
* Suggest savings tips

**Example AI output:**

> "You spent 18,000 BDT on average last 3 months.
> Your predicted expense next month is 20,500 BDT.
> Reason: rising food and transport cost."

---

## **🧠 (C) AI ChatBot (Financial Advisor)**

User can ask:

* “Why is my expense high this week?”
* “How can I save money?”
* “Show me my biggest spending category.”

AI replies with data from your app database.

---

## **🧠 (D) Smart Screenshot Scanner**

User uploads a bill or receipt → AI reads:

* Shop name
* Amount
* Category
* Auto-add as expense

You can use:

* **Tesseract OCR** (FREE & offline)
* Or Vision AI (optional)
